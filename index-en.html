What is Arbora ?

Arbora means trees — a fitting name for a brand that works with wood in all its forms. Based in Limburg, we craft beautiful wooden products by combining reclaimed old wood and fresh timber, creating pieces that last.

We restore and renew old furniture into striking new designs and make custom products with care and craftsmanship. Sustainability and quality are key, so every item tells a story and fits perfectly in your home or workplace.

Choose Arbora for authentic woodwork that brings nature inside.
