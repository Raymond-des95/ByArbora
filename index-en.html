What is Arbora?

Arbora is derived from the Latin word for trees. A name that perfectly reflects our craft and passion: creating unique products where wood takes center stage.

From Limburg, we design and create custom-made pieces for both interior and exterior spaces with care and craftsmanship. Whenever possible, we combine characterful reclaimed wood with high-quality new timber, giving every project its own identity and story. Whether it is a walnut dining table, a stylish custom cabinet, a complete interior project, or a unique piece of furniture, every design is created with attention to detail.

At Arbora, we believe wood is more than just a material. It brings warmth, character, and atmosphere into every space. That is why we choose sustainable materials, high-quality finishes, and solutions that are designed to last for years.

Our possibilities are extensive. From complete custom interiors and outdoor projects to tables, cabinets, lighting, planters, and other unique wooden creations. Do you have your own idea or a specific request? We are happy to think along with you and translate your vision into a design that perfectly matches your home, garden, or business.

Arbora stands for honest craftsmanship, natural materials, and custom work built to last for generations.

Featured projects

Every project tells its own story. From robust oak tables and custom cabinets to complete interior and exterior projects. Below you will find a selection of projects we are proud to have worked on.

Custom interior barbershop

At Welling's Barbershop in Maastricht, we created a unique interior. The design was created by DK-Design and brought to life by us. The result is a stylish and functional space that perfectly matches the vision of the barbershop.

Railway sleeper bench

Custom basement interior

A complete custom-designed basement, including stairs, walls, and cabinets. The result is a functional and stylish space that makes optimal use of the available area, with great attention to detail and finishing.
