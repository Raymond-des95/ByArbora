Our Story

Arbora was founded by Raymond and Daimy — a couple with a shared love for wood, nature, and meaningful craftsmanship. From their home and garden in Limburg, they started creating small wooden pieces using reclaimed materials and honest tools.

What began as a creative hobby quickly grew into a way of life: restoring old furniture, designing timeless wooden objects, and bringing new life into forgotten materials. Each piece is made with care, telling a story of simplicity, sustainability, and purpose.

Arbora stands for more than design — it's about slowing down, choosing quality, and staying close to the essence of things. Everything is handcrafted, locally made, and created with respect for nature and tradition.

Welcome to our journey.
